About us

Conveniently located at Mylapore in Kollam district, Travancore Medicity is a multi-specialty hospital that truly cares for life. A pioneer in the field of education and healthcare, We promise to deliver the highest level of professional expertise and world-class care. Deployment of ultra-modern technology further adds to its strength and commitment to give its patients the best of healthcare. The quality domains apply to all staff and departments. Travancore Medicity strives for excellence in healthcare by encouraging a culture of support, respect, integrity and teamwork; by monitoring and assessing the performance against national and international standards of care. By learning from successes and setbacks, Travancore Medicity endeavors to improve through innovation and change; and works in partnership and collaboration with all local agencies of health and social care.

Cardiology

Division of Clinical Cardiology offers the crux of cardiology services headed by experienced cardiologists of the state with a wide variant of diagnostic modalities and treatment options. This division handles Electrophysiology, Cardiology Clinic, Wellness and Lifestyle Cardiology Clinic.

Neurology

Department of Neurology at Travancore Medicity addresses various disorders pertaining to the entire nervous system including the brain, spinal cord, peripheral nerves and extra- cranial cerebrovascular system. The department offers comprehensive consultative services, diagnostic testing, and treatment for a broad spectrum of neurological conditions. Neurosurgery involves Microsurgery and Minimally Invasive Endoscopic Surgery.

Endocrinology

Combining clinical expertise with a patient-centered approach, the Diabetes and Endocrinology specialists of TRAVANCORE MEDICITY focuses on the treatment and prevention of diabetes and other endocrine conditions, including metabolic syndrome, osteoporosis, and pituitary and thyroid disorders.

Neonatology

The Neonatology department in Travancore Medicityis well known for medical care in newborn infants having low birth weight, intrauterine growth retardation, congenital malformations (birth defects), sepsis, or birth asphyxias.

Oncology

he Department of Medical Oncology at Travancore Medicity provides medical expertise for the treatment and prevention of solid tumours in adults and children
